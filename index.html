About Me


Welcome to my website! As most of you already know, my name is Philip. I'm an Accountant by trade, a hopeful coder during
the day and a keen guitarist by night.

I've been a self-taught fingerstyle guitarist for many years now and always long for the day I get to sit down and dabble on my
instrument after a hard week's worth of work!

Here you will find all things musical - from peaceful classical ensembles to bone crunching heavy metal. This website has not only
been created as way for you to get to know me better but, also a way to build a community of both musicians and those who just love
listening to music!
